The Bible is the word of God, according to Christians. Therefore, reading the Bible is one of the most important parts of being a Christian. It is, however, not easy, especially for young people. These dope Bible quotes will encourage youngsters to read the Good Book.

Many young people struggle to find time for reading the Bible. Those who have the time are not sure where to start. Send them these Bible quotes to get them started and stay interested in the word of God.
