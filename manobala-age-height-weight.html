Manobala Age, Height, Weight: Born on December 8, 1953, Manobala was one of the most popular comedians in the South Indian cinema industry.

He was an Indian film director, producer, actor, comedian, and YouTuber who predominantly played supporting roles in Tamil-language films.

Manobala, though known for his comedic roles, was a renowned director back in the 80s and 90s directing several films and movies.

He has directed Rajinikanth, Radhika, Suhasini, Prabhu, Sathyaraj, and Sivaji Ganesan, to mention a few.

Oorkavalan, En Purushanthaan Enakku Mattumthaan, and Dhoorathu Pachai are some of his popular films.

Manobala has also shared screen space with leading actors in Tamil cinema like Dhanush, Suriya, Vijay, Karthi, and Jaya Ravi.

He’s best known for his roles in Yaaradi Nee Mohini, Siruthai, and Nanban. Though Manobala played small roles in films, he made a huge impact and garnered widespread popularity.

The director-turned-actor and producer, Manobala was last in the Tamil films ‘Kondraal Paavam’ and ‘Ghosty’.

Sadly, the popular comedian and character artist has died. He passed away at the age of 69 on Wednesday, April 3, 2023, at his residence in Chennai due to liver-related problems.

Earlier, he was earlier admitted to Apollo Hospital in Chennai and was later shifted to his residence.

Manobala was unwell for the past fifteen days and stopped responding to treatment and breathed his last this afternoon (May 3, 2023).

Prior to his death, he had also undergone an angio-treatment in January after experiencing chest pain.

At the time of this report, the family was planning his final funeral arrangement and the details are yet to be finalized. We will keep you updated.
