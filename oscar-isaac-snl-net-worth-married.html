Oscar Isaac is set to make his hosting debut on Saturday Night Live in March, along with Zoe Kravitz. Saturday Night Live announced that both the actors would be hosting the sketch comedy show starting next month, and Oscar will lead the 5 March episode.

Singer Charlie XCX will appear on the show as the musical guest in the episode, which will be Oscar's hosting debut show. She was supposed to perform on the show back in December, but her performance was canceled as the show scaled back due to the Covid-19 omicron variant. She had first appeared on the show back in 2014, so the episode of Oscar's hosting debut will be her comeback on the show.

Rosalia will be joining Kravitz's hosting debut show in SNL as the musical guest on 12 March.

Net Worth Of Oscar Isaac

Oscar Isaac has an estimated net worth of $10 million. He is one of the well-respected and dignified American actors, and reportedly, he was paid $130,000 for his role in The Nativity Story in 2006. However, there has been a record of his earning just $2000 for his role in All About the Benjamins in 2002.

His earnings have been increased throughout the years, and he was paid approximately $2 million in Star Wars: The Force Awakens for his role as Poe Dameron.

Oscar Isaac was born in 1979 in Guatemala City, Guatemala, and moved to Florida, US, when he was five years old with his family. He made his debut in a crime drama, Milltown, in the mid-90s and acted in mid-200s films like All About the Benjamins, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, and more.

His breakthrough role was in The Star Wars: The Force Awakens, X Men: The Apocalypse, Show Me a Hero, A Most Violent Year, Ex Machina, and more. Some of his films are Balibo, Che, Robin Hood, Drive, W.E, ten years, The Life Before Her Eyes, Body of Lies, Agora, and more.

He has won the Australian Film Institute Award for Best Supporting Actor, A Golden Globe Award, and more. He is also a skilled musician and guitarist.
