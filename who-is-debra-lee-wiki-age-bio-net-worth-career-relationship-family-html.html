Debra Lee’s Career Highlights

  • Debra Lee assumed the role of President and Chief Operating Officer (COO) of BET Holdings, Inc. in March 1996, succeeding the network founder, Robert L. Johnson.
  • In 2005, she was promoted to the position of President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO).
  • Under her leadership, Black Entertainment Television experienced significant growth, reaching nearly $100 million in revenue last year.
  • Lee revolutionized the network by increasing production by 50% and introducing original programming with 16 new shows for the 2007 season.
  • She revamped BET’s mission, focusing on supporting families, nurturing dreams, and showcasing fresh talent through new shows.
  • In September 2009, she created CENTRIC, a new entertainment network featuring new artists, reality shows, and movies.
